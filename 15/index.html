RATIONALE

I exhibited five artworks that relate to my theme, “Memories.” I used three types of media within this exhibition artwork (3D,2D,digital).I have done this to show my diverse background.By doing this I also intended to make the exhibition interesting since rather than just having one media, several media will be more interesting. I tried to make all the artworks unite by making short spaces between artworks in different artforms. I also chose artworks that selected artworks that had bright colors so that even though they are all indifferent mediums, the impression of the artworks are the same and make sure it is not awkward to have different media in the same area.

Through my exhibition, I aim to show myself through my artwork relating to my memories self expression using artwork.

WORKS

Connection

Connection

MEDIUM

Color pencil

DIMENSIONS

35 × 25 cm

CAPTION

I focused on the social connection between humans and the environment. The connection between the theme and my memories is choosing the sea as the environment connecting to it. When I was little,I went to the beach many times with my family and I remember that going to the sea was an extraordinary experience that really made me satisfied. To show my personality as an optimistic person within the artwork, I tried to use bright colors.

The Protector

The Protector

MEDIUM

Mixed-media

DIMENSIONS

15 × 15 × 25 cm

CAPTION

This artwork is a container for my memorable things such as my photo when I was a baby, a shiny medal with a happy birthday message written by my mom etc. The things which were in the container are very important to me so I did not display them. Although those things are not displayed, the container itself embodies my memories. The pictures which were all taken by me or my family members in the past are used to collage the tail of the creature that is designed on the container. I chose to use this creature(Shisa; an imaginary creature in Okinawa) because it expresses my memories of going on family trips and the creature is considered a protector in Okinawa which means it can protect my memories safely.
